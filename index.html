The beginner problem

Most new traders stare at charts without a clear process.

They chase green candles after the move already happened.

They buy without knowing where the trade idea is invalid.

They confuse random signals with a repeatable setup.

They risk too much before learning the basics.

The software

A simple chart assistant for learning trade setups.

The tool is designed as an educational assistant, not a magic signal machine. It helps beginners slow down, check context, and understand why a setup might or might not be valid.

↗

Trend direction

Uses simple moving-average style logic to help users understand whether price is trending, ranging, or losing momentum.

▰

Support & resistance

Highlights possible zones where price previously reacted, so beginners can plan instead of guessing.

⚠

Risk reminders

Prompts users to think about invalidation, stop-loss placement, position size, and avoiding overtrading.
